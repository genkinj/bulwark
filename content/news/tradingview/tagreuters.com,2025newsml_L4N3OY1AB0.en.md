\*\* Shares in German speciality packaging maker Gerresheimer jumped up as much as 15% after Bloomberg News published a report saying the company is exploring a potential sale

\*\* According to the report Gerresheimer is in contact with private equity funds, among them Warburg Pincus, EQT AB and KKR & Co

\*\* The company did not immediately respond to Reuters' request for comment

\*\* Shares are on track for their best day since May 2024, if gains hold
